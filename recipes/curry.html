World's Cheapest Easiest Curry

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Thai curry doesn't need any bells or whistles to taste great.

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 bag of frozen stir fry veggies
  • Shredded chicken (rotisserie chicken works great, otherwise you will have to cook it beforehand)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 Tbsp Thai Red Curry Paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • Soy sauce (to taste)
  • 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper
  • butter or oil
  1. Cook rice according to the directions on the back of the bag (or box if you are using instant rice).
  2. Cook garlic and veggies in a large pan over medium-high heat with butter or oil. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Add cooked chicken to the large pot.
  4. Add coconut milk and water. Heat 5-10 minutes.
  5. Add Thai red curry paste and stir.
  6. Remove from heat and add soy sauce and lime to preferred taste.

World's Cheapest Easiest Butter Chicken

Total Time: 30 min

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 20 min

One of the more involved recipes on this site ingredients-wise. But probably still worth trying. Garam Masala isn't cheap, but you can use it to make this dish like 1000 times.

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1lb chicken thighs, cut into small chunks
  • 2 Tbsp Garam Masala
  • 6 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 cup of full-fat plain yogurt (or 1/2 cup of heavy cream if you prefer)
  • vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 14 oz of canned crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Cook white rice according to directions on the bag.
  2. In a large pan, heat 1 Tbsp of oil. Then add salt and peppered chicken thigh chunks. Cook until brown on the outside and cooked through (~5 min).
  3. Add garlic, onions, tomatoes and garam masala. Cook until soft. (~ 5 min depending on the veggies).
  4. Add yogurt, salt and pepper and stir until hot and well-blended.
  5. Serve over rice.

World's Cheapest Easiest "Lo Mein"

Total Time: 20 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Not quite Lo Mein but it sure tastes great.

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Green pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Chicken thighs
  • Sesame oil (1 Tbsp)
  • Soy sauce (to taste)
  • Sriracha
  • Salt and pepper
  1. In a pot, boil salted water.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles to the boiling water and cook until soft (~10 minutes).
  3. Meanwhile, cut chicken thighs into small pieces. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Add chicken and sesame oil to a large pan on medium-high heat until outside is starting to brown and inside is cooked through (5-10 minutes depending on how large the chunks are).
  5. Add garlic, mushrooms and sliced green pepper into the pan and cook until soft (~ 5 min).
  6. Combine cooked spaghetti noodles with chicken and veggies mix.
  7. Add soy sauce, Sriracha and additional sesame oil to preferred taste.

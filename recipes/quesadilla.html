World's Cheapest Easiest Quesadilla

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

The cheapest and easiest of all cheap and easy recipes.

  • 2 flour quesadillas
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • pick your protein: beans, shredded chicken, or ground beef
  • butter
  1. Between two flour tortillas, fill with shredded cheese.
  2. Add protein (beans or meat).
  3. (Optional) Add vegetables or other condiments (spinach, mushrooms, and chipotle mayo are three of my favs).
  4. Melt butter in pan over medium heat.
  5. Place quesadilla on buttered pan. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Add more butter to the side of the quesadilla not on the pan.
  7. Flip and cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  8. Cut into triangles to make it look fancy. I swear it tastes better.

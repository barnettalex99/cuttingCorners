World's Cheapest Easiest Enchiladas

Total Time: 25 min

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 15 min

More easy mexican food. Anything in a casserole dish is great meal prep.

  • Flour tortillas
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • pick your protein: beans, shredded chicken, or ground beef
  • Canned corn
  • Large can of enchilada sauce (red or green)
  • Sour cream other extras (greek yogurt is a good a substitute if you have that already)
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Inside each tortilla, put one spoonful of beans, meat and corn. Roll and place side by side in a baking dish.
  3. Cover with enchilada sauce.
  4. Cover with shredded cheese.
  5. Cook in the oven until the cheese is melted and insides are warm (10-15 min).
  6. Add whatever you want on top (sour cream, guac, jalepenos, whatever warms your heart).

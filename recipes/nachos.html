World's Cheapest Easiest Nachos

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

In case you have some leftover ingredients from your quesadillas.

  • Tortilla chips
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • pick your protein: beans, shredded chicken, or ground beef
  • Pick your veggies: tomatoes, kale, peppers, etc.
  • Sour cream other extras (greek yogurt is a good a substitute if you have that already)
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cook your protein and veggies (unless you are using leftovers, then just toss them on)
  3. Cover with shredded cheese.
  4. Cook in the oven until the cheese is melted.
  5. Add whatever you want on top (sour cream, guac, jalepenos, whatever warms your heart).

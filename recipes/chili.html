World's Cheapest Easiest Chili

Total Time: 25 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 20 min

You can make enough chili to feed an army with this recipe.

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 2 15 oz cans of diced tomatoes
  • 2 16 oz cans of kidney beans
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • Sour cream, cheese, guac or other extras (greek yogurt is a good a substitute for sour cream if you have that already)
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil.
  2. Cook your onion and ground beef (6-7 min).
  3. Add chili powder, tomatoes, beans and broth. Stir and cook until warm all the way through (10-15 min).
  4. Add whatever you want on top (cheese, sour cream, guac, jalepenos, whatever warms your heart).

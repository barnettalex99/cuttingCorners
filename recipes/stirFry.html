World's Cheapest Easiest Stir Fry

Total Time: 20 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 20 min

Sick of eating our Lo Mein? Put those same ingredients over rice to make a delicious stir fry.

  • Frozen stir fry veggies (or whatever fresh veggies you want to add)
  • 1lb chicken thighs, cut into small chunks
  • Soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • Chili garlic sauce or Sriracha
  • Sesame oil
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Cook white rice according to directions on the bag.
  2. In a large pan, heat 1 Tbsp of sesame oil. Then add salt and peppered chicken thigh chunks. Cook until brown on the outside and cooked through (~5 min).
  3. Add garlic and veggies. Cook until soft. (~ 5 min depending on the veggies).
  4. Add soy sauce, Sriracha or chili garlic sauce and additional sesame oil to preferred taste.
  5. Serve over rice.

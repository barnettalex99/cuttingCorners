World's Cheapest Easiest Buffalo Chicken Dip

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

People will beg you to bring this to the Super Bowl.

  • 1 20 oz can of white meat chicken chunks
  • 2 8 oz package of cream cheese/li>
  • 1 cup of store brand buffalo or 'wing sauce'
  • 1 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Heat chicken and buffalo sauce in a skillet over medium heat until heated through (3-5 min).
  2. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing.
  3. Sprinkle in cheddar cheese and stir until everything is melted, well blended and warm.
  4. Serve with celery, carrots, or chips. Put it in lettuce wraps or sandwiches. Eat it straight out of the pan. Enjoy.

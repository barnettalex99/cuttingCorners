World's Cheapest Easiest Grilled Cheese

Total Time: 10 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 5 min

One of the cases where the cheapest, easiest version also happens to be the best.

  • Kraft singles American cheese slices
  • White bread
  • Butter
  1. Melt butter in a pan on medium-low heat.
  2. Put 2 slices of kraft singles between 2 slices of white bread.
  3. Place sandwich on buttered pan and cook until golden brown.
  4. As you flip the sandwich, add more butter to the pan and ensure that the bottom of the sandwich is coated.
  5. Cook until the other side is golden brown and cheese is melted.
  6. Serve with Campbell's tomato soup.

World's Cheapest Easiest Mediterranean Nachos

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Feel free to put this in a pita wrap if that makes your heart sing, but I find nachos so fun to eat.

  • Pita chips
  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1 green pepper, cut in slices
  • Greek yogurt
  1. Cook the sausage in a skillet over medium heat until brown and crumbling.
  2. Cover pita chips with sausage, hummus and green peppers.
  3. Top with a dollop of Greek yogurt.

World's Cheapest Easiest Meatball Subs

Total Time: 30 min

Prep Time: 20 min

Cook Time: 10 min

A childhood classic.

  • Cooked frozen meatballs
  • Pasta sauce
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Hoagie roll
  1. Fill hoagie roll with cooked frozen meatballs. Top with pasta sauce and cheese.
  2. Put in the oven at 350 F for 10 minutes.

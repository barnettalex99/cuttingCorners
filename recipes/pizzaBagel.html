World's Cheapest Easiest Pizza Bagel

Total Time: 30 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

A childhood classic.

  • Bagel, halved
  • Pasta sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  1. Top each halved bagel with pasta sauce, cheese and pepperoni (and any other toppings that you'd like.)
  2. Put in the oven at 350 F for 10 minutes.

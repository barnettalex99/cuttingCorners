World's Cheapest Easiest Pasta Sauce

Total Time: 15 min

Prep Time: 5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Get rid of your leftovers by adding literally anything to pasta.

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 can tomato sauce (35 oz)
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • Whatever protein you have lying around
  • Whatever veggies you have lying around.
  • Salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  1. In a pot, boil salted water.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles to the boiling water and cook until soft (~10 minutes).
  3. Add garlic and oil to a large saucepan over medium heat and cook until soft.
  4. Add leftover meat and veggies. Warm 5 minutes.
  5. Add canned tomato sauce and heat until simmering.
  6. Remove from heat and add salt and pepper to preferred taste.
